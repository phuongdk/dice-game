GAME RULES

  • - The game has 2 players, play in a round
  • - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. Each result get added to his ROUND SCORE
  • - BUT, if the player rolls a DICE 1(applied to both dices), all his ROUND SCORE gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • - AND, if the player rolls TWO DICE SIX IN A ROW(applied to dice one only), all his ROUND SCORE and GLOBAL SCORE gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • - The player can choose to 'Preserve', which means that his ROUND SCORE gets added to his GLOBAL SCORE. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • - The first player to reach WINNING SCORE on GLOBAL SCORE wins the game
